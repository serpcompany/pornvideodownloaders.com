OnlyFans Bulk Downloader

serp.ly/onlyfans-bulk-downloader

Bulk download photos and videos from OnlyFans creators. Rate-limited queue. Chat media. In-page buttons. Free trial included.

Overview

OnlyFans Bulk Downloader is a browser extension that batch-downloads photos, videos, and chat media from your OnlyFans subscriptions. Select the creators you subscribe to, choose your media type, and the extension downloads everything to your computer — organized, rate-limited, and queue-managed. No external software needed.

Why It Exists

Purpose-built for OnlyFans with multi-creator bulk download, configurable rate limiting (conservative to aggressive), persistent download queue with pause/resume/retry, in-page download buttons on every image and video, chat media support, and media type filtering. Everything runs in your browser with smart rate limiting to protect your account.

Offline use cases

  • Bulk download all photos and videos from one or more OnlyFans creators you subscribe to
  • Download only photos or only videos with the media type filter
  • Save chat/DM media from conversations
  • Use in-page overlay buttons for quick one-off downloads while browsing
  • Manage large download queues with pause, resume, and retry
  • Control download speed to avoid triggering platform rate limits

FAQ

How does bulk downloading work?

After setup, select one or more creators from your OnlyFans subscriptions. The extension fetches their posts via the OnlyFans API, extracts all media URLs (photos and videos), and queues them for download. The queue processes files with your chosen rate limit preset.

Will this get my OnlyFans account banned?

The extension includes three rate limiter presets specifically designed to reduce risk. The Conservative preset (20-30 files/min) is the safest. We recommend using the Balanced preset (40-50 files/min) as a starting point. The extension also includes random timing jitter to simulate human-like behavior.

Can I download from any OnlyFans creator?

You can only download from creators you are actively subscribed to. The extension uses your existing OnlyFans session and only accesses content your account has permission to view.

What media types can I download?

Photos (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, AVIF) and videos (MP4, MOV, WebM, and more). You can filter to download only photos, only videos, or all media.

Can I download chat/DM media?

Yes. The extension includes dedicated chat selection and chat list pages for browsing and downloading media from your OnlyFans conversations.

Where are my downloads saved?

All files automatically save to an OnlyFans subfolder inside your browser's default Downloads directory.

Can I pause and resume downloads?

Yes. The download queue supports pause, resume, stop, cancel all, clear finished, and retry cancelled operations. You can also adjust concurrency (1-8 simultaneous downloads) while the queue is running.

What are the download speed presets?

Conservative (2-3s delay, 25 req/min — safest), Balanced (1-1.5s delay, 45 req/min — recommended), Aggressive (0.5-1s delay, 70 req/min — fastest but riskiest).

Does this work on Firefox?

Yes — supports Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Whale, Yandex, and Firefox.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All downloads happen directly between your browser and the OnlyFans CDN. Extension authentication uses secure OTP — no OnlyFans password is accessed or stored.
