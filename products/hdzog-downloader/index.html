Overview

Video Downloader for HDZog is a browser extension that saves videos from HDZog.com to your computer. Play a video, click the download button on the player or the extension icon, pick your quality, and it saves as MP4. No extra software needed. 3 free downloads included.

Why It Exists

Purpose-built for HDZog with multi-source video detection (flashvars, HTML5 video, player source extraction), in-page download button, and automatic HLS-to-MP4 conversion. Everything happens in your browser — no external tools required.

Offline use cases

  • Save a video from HDZog for offline viewing
  • Download in your preferred quality
  • Build a personal library
  • Use the in-page player button for one-click downloads without the popup
  • Right-click any video to download via context menu

FAQ

How do I download a video?

Go to any video page on HDZog.com and press play. Then click the download button on the player, click the extension icon, or right-click and select "Download HDZog Video."

What quality options are available?

The extension detects all available qualities from the source — typically multiple resolutions. Formats are sorted by quality (highest first), with MP4 preferred over HLS.

What format are downloaded videos?

All videos are saved as standard MP4 files that play on any device or media player.

Where are my downloads saved?

Videos automatically save to an HDZog subfolder inside your browser's default Downloads directory.

How many free downloads do I get?

3 free trial downloads after signing in with your email. Purchase a license for unlimited downloads.

Does this work on Firefox?

Yes — supports Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Whale, Yandex, and Firefox.

Why isn't the extension finding my video?

Press play on the video first. The extension needs the stream to start before detection works. If issues persist, refresh the page.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Video processing happens entirely in your browser. Authentication uses secure OTP — no passwords stored.
